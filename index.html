What is Revibe?

Revibe is an AI-based personal rehabilitation assistant that makes recovery more efficient, affordable, and sustainable. Using advanced motion recognition technology, Revibe provides real-time feedback and personalized exercise guidance, helping you regain strength and vitality through every step of your recovery journey.
